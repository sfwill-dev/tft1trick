My TFT climb started in Emerald, where I was inconsistent and tilted easily after low rolls.

Over four sets, I committed to a one-trick learning process:

1. Review every Stage 3 and Stage 4 loss
2. Track item slams that stabilized boards
3. Build default lines for contested and uncontested games

That structure took me from reactive play to deliberate decision-making, and eventually to Masters.

This site documents the same process so other players can shorten their learning curve.
