## 🥇 Void Kai'Sa 4 Bruisers

![Void Kai'Sa 4 Bruisers board](/comps/patch-16.8-void-kaisa-4bruisers.png)

Yep, you read that right, same comp as last patch. I've been testing Set 17 on PBE more than playing 16.8, but this is still my pick for the best one-trick of the patch, and still strong enough to climb.

**Why I'm picking this comp to one-trick this patch**:
- Strong enough to climb, solid average placement in the current data.
- No hard requirements.
  - Unlocks: Since patch 16.6, Kai'Sa and Herald unlock conditions have been much more accessible, which makes the comp reliable to one-trick, as it consistently spikes on level 7.
    - Kai'Sa: <s>3 items equipped on a Longshot + Level 7</s> ⇒ 2 items on a Longshot + Level 7
    - Rift Herald: <s>Field Void for 8 combats</s> ⇒ Field Void for 2 combats + Level 7
  - Items: Kai'Sa has two distinct builds, AP or AD, so you can commit to whichever your early items point toward and start slamming, just don't mix them (see recommended items below).
- Flexible board: play the 4 core void units (Kai'Sa, Cho, Herald, Kog'Maw) alongside whatever you hit: 1 Jugg + 3 Bruisers, 1 Bruiser + 3 Juggs, or even 1 Jugg + 1 Bruiser + 2 generic tanks (Taric, Skarner), until the ideal units show up.
- It's a Fast 8 comp, so there are no Reroll or Fast 9 conditions to meet.

**Gameplan**:
Play around a strong opener, hold items for Kai'Sa (AP or AD depending on what drops), and save HP until your level 7 spike.

If it's shaping up to be a level 10 game (econ encounter, prismatic econ augment, etc.), you can pivot to the [Baron version](#9baron), high risk / high reward: better odds at top 1/2, but a failed hit means bot 4. If I just see the angle, I prefer to stay on the regular Kai'Sa comp and hold void units on the bench without ruining my econ.

**Stage 1-2**: Play strongest board. Lose-streaking isn't ideal, but if your opener is weak, take an econ augment if possible, prioritize econ, and aim for the level 7 spike.

**Stage 3**:Get 2 void units on your board by 3-3 at the latest, they need two rounds on the board to unlock Herald. Level to 7 on 3-5 to unlock Kai'Sa and Herald, setting up wins on 3-6 and 4-1. If you're rich, leveling earlier nets you more stage 3 wins.

**Stage 4**: Level to 8 ideally on 4-2 and roll for your final board (except Ziggs, who comes in at 9).

**Stage 5**: Level to 9 ideally on 5-2 and unlock Ziggs. You can do that by using leftover items or a TG on a Zaunite or a Yordle (such as Kobuko & Yuumi, already on board), or even a temporary Teemo swapped out next round so you don't waste removers.

**Tips**:

- Saving the game when things go wrong:
  - A 2-star 4-cost tank is often more urgent than a 2-star Kai'Sa. If you 2-star Swain or Wukong before Rift Herald, itemize them. If you're somehow contested on all three (Herald, Swain, Wukong), drop to 2 bruisers and play Taric + Skarner and itemize whichever you hit first.
  - If still struggling to 2-star Kai'Sa level 9, on the AP version her items can be put on a 2-star Ziggs. On the AD version, a 2-star Lucian or Senna might be an option to hold them instead.
- Always pick augments based on your specific spot, but item augments are generally strong here, Kai'Sa's items are specific, and you have four units worth itemizing: Kai'Sa, Rift Herald, Volibear, and Ziggs.
- AP Kai'Sa is auto-attack based and wants Rageblade + AP.
- AD Kai'Sa is a caster and wants Shojin + IE.

**Early/mid game ideal item holders**:

- Kog'Maw -> AP Kai'Sa
- Caitlyn -> AD Kai'Sa
- Cho'Gath -> Rift Herald

**Recommended Items**:

**AP Kai'Sa**: Rageblade, Jeweled Gauntlet, Gunblade<br/>
Attack speed + AP

**AD Kai'Sa**: Shojin, Infinity Edge, Last Whisper<br/>
Mana + AD

**Rift Herald**: Generic tank

**Ziggs**: Leftover AP, utility, Rageblade

### Variations:

### Void Kai'Sa 4 Juggernauts

![Void Kai'Sa 4 Jugg board](/comps/patch-16.8-void-kaisa-4jugg.png)

Same as the 4 Bruisers version, but swap Volibear + Wukong + Kobuko & Yuumi (Ziggs' Yordle trait) for Shyvana + Singed + Dr. Mundo (Ziggs' Zaun trait).

This version tends to cap higher, but for me the difference rarely justifies holding units and ruining econ to force it, as Volibear and Kobuko are not hard to unlock with 4 bruisers on 8, while Shyvana usually only shows up on 9.
That said, if you highroll Shyvana before unlocking Volibear, or 2-star Volibear is heavily contested, you can unlock Singed and play this version.
